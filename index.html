Clinician-authored AI
The AI reasoning layer is authored and signed off by named consultant surgeons. Expert clinical logic, formalised, versioned, and traceable. Not prompted by engineers — built by the clinicians who own the decision.
⚡
Two clinical layers, not one
Deterministic hard rules handle the obvious cases — the AI cannot override them. AI reasoning handles the uncertain middle: the vague, chronic, low-risk patient where a decision tree fails and a clinician needs genuine support.
📋
Guideline-based, evidence-layered
Grounded in NICE NG12. Weighted by the best available FIT, colonoscopy, and symptom combination evidence. Deflecting logic for vague presentations — safely reducing referrals without missing cancers.
📁
Regulatory-grade from day one
Every decision logged, versioned, and traceable. Validated against a clinical vignette set. Built for MHRA registration and NHS clinical governance from the start. The audit trail is the research dataset.

The hardest referral decisions in primary care aren't the obvious ones — they're the uncertain ones. Hummingbird is built specifically for those moments.
